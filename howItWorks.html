Self driving cars work through 5 main components:

  1. Computer Vision
    • How we use camera images to figure out what the world around us looks like.
  2. Sensor Fusion
    • How we incorporate data from other sensors like lasers and radar.
  3. Localization
    • Figure out PRECISELY where we are in the world.
  4. Path Planning
    • Chart a course through the world to get us to where we'd like to go.
  5. Car Control
    • How we actually push the throttle, push the brake, or turn the steering wheel.
