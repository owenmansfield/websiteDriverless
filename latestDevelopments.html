The training of deep neural networks (deep learning) has propelled the field forward in recent years. Neural networks are programs that can actually "learn", or optimize their parameters, based on past experience and inputs.

Another important recent technological improvement has been in the area of localization. While GPS is great to get a location accurate to 1-2 meters... if a car is wrong about where it is by 1-2 meters, the results could be catastrophic! So, using high definition maps and "particle filters" - or advanced triangulation by measuring distances from various landmarks - self-driving cars can localize to within 1cm of their actual location!
